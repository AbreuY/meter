Meter is a live wallpaper that visualizes your battery, wireless signal and notifications in a simple wallpaper visualization. It cycles through three visualizations with every unlock and you can play with each wallpaper by tilting and moving the phone.

Original design and concept by Mikkel Closer with code by Jonas Jongejan and Kyle Phillips

Meter is a part of Android Experiments and is open-source. Checkout Meter's code on Github.
